About RCPD

Welcome to our RCPD resource website. Our mission is to provide reliable information and tools to help those affected by Retrograde Cricopharyngeal Dysfunction (RCPD) – a condition that prevents the normal release of gas through burping.

We created this site because we believe that awareness and proper diagnosis are essential for better treatment and management. Our goal is to connect patients with accurate information, self-diagnosis tools, research, and qualified specialists.
