What is RCPD?

RCPD stands for Retrograde Cricopharyngeal Dysfunction. It's also known as the No-Burp Syndrome. It’s a condition where the cricopharyngeus muscle in the throat fails to relax properly, preventing a natural burp. This can result in chronic bloating, discomfort, and gurgling noises as trapped air cannot escape.
