Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is RCPD?

    RCPD stands for Retrograde Cricopharyngeal Dysfunction, a condition where the throat muscle does not relax properly, leading to difficulty in burping.

  • How do I know if I have RCPD?

    We provide a self-diagnosis tool on the Diagnosis page. Remember, this tool is for informational purposes only and is not a substitute for professional medical advice.

  • What treatment options are available?

    Treatment options vary depending on the individual. Consult a specialist for a personalized treatment plan.
